    <div>
      <h3 class="text-purple-900 font-bold text-xl uppercase tracking-tight">Clinical Impact</h3>
      <p class="text-gray-600 leading-relaxed text-lg">
        Gout is the <span class="text-purple-700 font-semibold underline decoration-purple-100 decoration-4 underline-offset-4">most prevalent inflammatory arthritis</span>, affecting 2%–5% of the population worldwide.
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="flex items-start">
    <div class="text-purple-700 font-black text-4xl mr-6 opacity-20">02</div>
    <div>
      <h3 class="text-purple-900 font-bold text-xl uppercase tracking-tight">Cardiovascular Burden</h3>
      <p class="text-gray-600 leading-relaxed text-lg">
        It is associated with a <span class="text-purple-700 font-bold uppercase">1.5-fold increased risk</span> of cardiovascular (CV) events, with inflammation during flares being a potential key driver.
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="flex items-center space-x-3 mb-10">
  <div class="w-1.5 h-8 bg-purple-700"></div>
  <h1 class="text-4xl font-bold text-gray-800">The Core Controversy</h1>
</div>

<div class="grid grid-cols-2 gap-8 mt-6">
  <div class="p-6 rounded-2xl bg-purple-50 border border-purple-100 relative shadow-sm">
    <div class="absolute -top-4 left-6 bg-purple-700 text-white px-3 py-1 text-xs font-bold rounded">KNOWLEDGE GAP</div>
    <p class="text-gray-700 leading-loose">
      While ULT effectively reduces gout flares by targeting <span class="text-purple-800 font-bold text-lg">SU < 6 mg/dL</span>, its direct impact on <b>MACE</b> reduction remains unproven and controversial.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="p-6 rounded-2xl bg-gray-50 border border-gray-100 relative shadow-sm text-gray-400">
    <div class="absolute -top-4 left-6 bg-gray-400 text-white px-3 py-1 text-xs font-bold rounded">PREVIOUS TRIALS</div>
    <p class="text-sm leading-relaxed italic">
      RCTs like <b>ALL-HEART</b> and Mendelian randomization studies have shown inconsistent or no causal association between SU and CV outcomes.
    </p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="mt-12 p-6 border-t-2 border-purple-600">
  <h3 class="text-purple-900 font-bold text-xl mb-2 italic">Study Objective:</h3>
  <p class="text-gray-700 text-lg">
    To evaluate whether achieving the <span class="text-purple-700 font-bold">T2T SU target (< 6 mg/dL)</span> is associated with a lower risk of cardiovascular events over a 5-year follow-up.
  </p>
</div>
